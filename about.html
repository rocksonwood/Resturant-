Our Story

Founded in 2018, Saveur was born from a passion for creating extraordinary culinary experiences that challenge conventions while honoring tradition.

Our journey began when acclaimed chef Sophie Laurent and restaurateur James Owens joined forces with a vision to create a space where innovative gastronomy could flourish in an atmosphere of warmth and sophistication.

Our Philosophy

At Saveur, we believe in the transformative power of food. Our approach is guided by three core principles that inform everything we do.

Sustainability

We source our ingredients locally and seasonally, working directly with farmers who share our commitment to sustainable practices.

Innovation

Our kitchen is a laboratory of creativity where traditional techniques meet cutting-edge culinary science.

Experience

Every element of Saveur is designed to create a holistic dining experience that engages all the senses.
